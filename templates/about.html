About LearnSight

Empowering Educators with Explainable AI

Our Mission

LearnSight was developed with a clear mission: to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to help educators identify at-risk students early and understand the factors influencing academic outcomes. We believe that by making AI predictions transparent and explainable, we can empower educators to make data-informed decisions that positively impact student success.

How LearnSight Works

1

Data Collection

LearnSight analyzes 16 key student features across demographics, behavior, psychology, and academic history to build a comprehensive profile.

2

AI Prediction

Our trained machine learning models (Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM) process the data to predict the student's final grade.

3

Explainable Insights

Using SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations), LearnSight reveals exactly which factors contributed to the prediction and why.

4

Actionable Recommendations

Based on the analysis, LearnSight provides personalized recommendations to help educators support each student effectively.

What is SHAP?

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) is a game-theoretic approach to explain the output of any machine learning model. It connects optimal credit allocation with local explanations, providing a unified measure of feature importance.

In LearnSight, SHAP values tell us how much each feature (like study hours, attendance, etc.) contributed to pushing the prediction higher or lower from the baseline. Positive SHAP values push the prediction toward higher grades, while negative values push toward lower grades.

Key Benefits:
  • Transparent and interpretable predictions
  • Consistent and mathematically grounded
  • Works with any machine learning model
  • Provides both global and local explanations
